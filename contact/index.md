## Visiting the lab and IMCM / MIMC platform

Our laboratory and the **IMCM / MIMC (Molecular Interactions and Materials Characterization) platform** are located on the Laval campus, where we host collaborators, students and industrial partners for structural biology, NMR spectroscopy, biophysics and biomolecular characterization projects.

For questions regarding:

- **Graduate studies (M.Sc. / Ph.D.) or postdoctoral positions** – please contact **Nicolas Doucet** directly.  
- **Platform access, sample measurements or service requests** – please contact the IMCM / MIMC team (see platform website) or the Doucet Lab for scientific support.

[Learn more about the IMCM / MIMC platform](https://www.plateformesafsb.inrs.ca/MIMC/index.html)
