Hieu is the **platform technician for the IMCM/MIMC facility**, where he coordinates instrument access, schedules academic and industrial client requests, and supports the operation of the platform’s biophysical and analytical instrumentation, including NMR spectroscopy.

Within the Doucet Lab, he contributes to the **nanobody project targeting human RNases**, assisting with protein preparation, biophysical assays, sample handling and experimental logistics.

His dual role places him at the interface of **platform operations**, **structural biology workflows**, and **applied molecular engineering**.
